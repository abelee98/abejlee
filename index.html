Q-learning for Stock Trading

This was a final project for CS182: Artificial Intelligence. My partner and I decided to implement Q-learning to produce an optimal action for a given stock. Produced left is an example with Amazon stock with an optimal action to buy.

I had fun on this one. This was made as a means for me to get more familiar with Swift, XCode, and iOS programming in general. I used to Weather Channel API to get the weather forecast for a given city. Users can see "Today's", "Tomorrow's", and a 10 day weather forecast.

Ketch'Up

My partners and I made this app for our CS50 final project. Users can sign in through Facebook. Then they can notify their friends what table number they are sitting at or can check see what what table their friends are at. We made this using Swift, Firebase, and the Facebook login API.
